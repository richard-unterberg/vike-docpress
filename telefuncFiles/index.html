telefuncFiles

Environment: server.

This is a beta feature.

If you don't use Telefunc's transformer on your server-side (see Docs > How It Works > Transformer) then you need to provide the list of your .telefunc.js files to Telefunc.

You do so by using:

  • config.telefuncFiles
  • config.root

See also config.root.
